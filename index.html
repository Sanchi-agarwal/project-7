Our History

began in 2012 as a small neighborhood store in Mumbai. With a vision to make essential and luxury items accessible under one roof, we’ve grown into one of India’s most loved online shopping destinations.

Today, we proudly serve millions of customers across the country with thousands of products delivered daily to their doorstep.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an unmatched shopping experience that blends variety, affordability, and fast delivery. Whether it's electronics, groceries, clothing, furniture, or cosmetics — sarvatra has it all.

We aim to empower shoppers with trusted products and seamless service at every step.
